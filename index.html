The Peculiar Pandas

Peculiar Pandas is an exclusive collection of 9,999 Panda NFT's. Peculiar Pandas aims to support endangered species through donating the proceeds from the mint and secondary market sales to organisations fighting for this cause.

Peculiar Pandas are unique and each one has their own clothing style, facial expression and personality.

We aim to create a positive community for all, so jump in our Discord and say Hello!

FAQ's

What are Peculiar Pandas?

Peculiar Pandas are randomly generated, non-fungible tokens, also known as an "NFT." There is 9999 different Peculiar Pandas and each Panda is completely unique. No two Peculiar Pandas are the same. The Peculiar Pandas were generated from over 100 different hand drawn traits. Peculiar Pandas are minted, stored, and traded on the Ethereum blockchain.

When is the mint date?

The official mint date has note been announced as of yet. Please join our discord to keep up-to date.

Why Peculiar Pandas?

Not only do Peculiar Pandas look awesome! But a percentage of the proceeds from minting and trading will go towards organisations who help endangered animals. We will also be adopting a Panda through WWF and providing the community with regular updates.

How many Peculiar Pandas are there?

There are 9999 Peculiar Pandas.

How can I mint a Peculiar Panda?

Purchase Ethereum on your cryptocurrency brokerage of choice and send it to your wallet. You will be able to link your wallet after the mint sale begins. Each Panda will be minted for .06 ETH during the presale and .07 ETH during the public sale using the ERC-721 standard.
